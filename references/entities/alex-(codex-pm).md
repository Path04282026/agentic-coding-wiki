# Alex (Codex PM)

**Role:** PM Lead for [Codex](references/entities/codex.md) at [OpenAI](references/entities/openai.md)
**Dimensions most associated with:** [06 - UI and Distribution](references/dimensions/06-ui-and-distribution.md), [07 - Feature Gating and Internals](references/dimensions/07-feature-gating-and-internals.md)

## Key Contributions

- Led the controversial decision to build the Codex App (when IDE extension was already popular)
- Articulated the "progressive disclosure" design philosophy for the Codex App
- Defined OpenAI's planning framework: near-term (≤ 8 weeks) or long-term (vibes), never medium-term
- Championed PM as "fill-in-the-gaps" not leadership — "It's a red flag if a startup has a PM when it's less than 20 engineers"

## Notable Quotes

> "How do we mostly let the product be almost invisible, get out of the way of the model."

> "You either plan near-term or long-term, but you never plan medium-term."

> "All of the lines between career ladders are blurring and we're all builders altogether."

> "We try to make it so it's almost like playing a game. You're just discovering what's next."
